Explore the heart of Transylvania

Welcome to Brașov

Brașov is a place with a lot going on for anyone traveling there. Historic spots and mountain views catch your eye right away, creating a strong first impression of both nature and culture. Whether you are interested in exploring the old town, trying local Romanian food, visiting nearby castles, or experiencing traditional villages, there is something for every kind of traveller. The city offers a mix of relaxation and activity, allowing visitors to enjoy peaceful walks through historic streets or take part in more adventurous experiences in the surrounding mountains. Its unique combination of scenery, history, and culture makes Brașov a destination that feels both welcoming and full of opportunities to explore.

Why Visit Brașov?

Whether you are planning a short weekend break or a longer holiday, Brașov offers a mix of relaxation, culture, and exploration. Visitors can spend time discovering the historic old town, enjoying traditional Romanian cuisine, or taking in the surrounding mountain landscapes. It is the kind of place where discovery happens naturally, and surprises are around every corner. From quiet walks through cobbled streets to more active experiences such as hiking or exploring nearby attractions, the city provides something for every pace of travel. This balance between calm and adventure makes Brașov an ideal destination for a wide range of visitors.

Highlights

  • Historic old town and medieval streets
  • Beautiful mountain scenery and outdoor activities
  • Traditional Romanian food and music, as well as local culture
  • Easy access to nearby castles and famous landmarks
  • Friendly atmosphere and welcoming local community
  • A perfect mix of nature, history, and modern city life
